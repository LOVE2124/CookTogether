Creamy Avocado Toast

⏱ 10 min 👤 Easy 🍽️ 2 servings 🔥 320 cal
★★★★★ 4.9 (248 reviews)

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 eggs (poached or fried)
  • Fresh microgreens for garnish
  • Extra virgin olive oil drizzle

📋 Instructions

  1. Toast the sourdough bread slices until golden and crispy.
  2. Halve the avocado, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mash with a fork to your desired consistency.
  4. Spread the avocado mixture generously over each toast slice.
  5. Top with a poached or fried egg for extra protein.
  6. Garnish with microgreens, red pepper flakes, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
