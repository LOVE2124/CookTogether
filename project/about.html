About CookTogether

We believe that food is the universal language of love. CookTogether is a community-powered platform where home cooks from around the world share their favorite recipes, stories, and culinary traditions.

Our Mission

CookTogether was founded in 2023 with a simple idea: everyone has a recipe worth sharing. Whether it's your grandmother's secret sauce, a quick weeknight dinner, or an adventurous weekend project — your recipe deserves an audience. We make it easy to share, discover, and save recipes that bring people together around the table.

📜 Community Guidelines

To keep CookTogether a welcoming and helpful space for everyone, please follow these guidelines:

  • ✅
    Share original recipes. Only submit recipes you have personally made and can vouch for. Credit inspiration sources when applicable.
  • 📸
    Use real photos. Upload your own photos of the finished dish. Stock photos or AI-generated images are not allowed.
  • 🤝
    Be respectful. Treat all community members with kindness. Constructive feedback is welcome; negativity is not.
  • 📋
    Write clear instructions. Make sure your steps are easy to follow for cooks of all skill levels.
  • 🏷️
    Tag accurately. Use correct categories, dietary labels (vegan, gluten-free, etc.), and difficulty levels.
  • 🚫
    No spam or self-promotion. Do not post promotional content, affiliate links, or unrelated advertisements.
  • ⚠️
    Report issues. If you see content that violates these guidelines, use the report button to notify our moderation team.
